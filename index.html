WebXR Snake Game

Experience the classic Snake game in Augmented Reality on your Meta Quest 3!

How to Play:

  1. Click "Start AR" to begin.
  2. Point your controller at a flat surface.
  3. Press the trigger to place the game board.
  4. Use the controller to guide the snake.
  5. Collect red spheres to grow and increase your score.
  6. Avoid collisions with walls and yourself.
